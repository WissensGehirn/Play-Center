🎮 Play Center

Your personal game launcher for Windows

What is Play Center?

Play Center is a simple and flexible game launcher for Windows. Add your .exe files or shortcuts and launch your favorite games conveniently from one place.

Functions

  • 🎯 Support for .exe and .lnk files
  • 🖼️ GameNames
  • 📝 Local game storage
  • 🚀 Quick start directly from the app
